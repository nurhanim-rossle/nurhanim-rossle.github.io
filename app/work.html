Level Up KL (levelupkl.com)

2016-2017

Level Up KL is Southeast Asia's premier industry event for game developers. They become a hub for gathering local and international game developers to interact, learn and share knowledge. I helped them created a website based on the design by their designer.

The website was created to display relevant information regarding their main event, which is Level Up KL. There are few hiccups along the way because some of the designs are not mobile web friendly, but we managed to work out a solution that both worked best and compliment their branding.

I was greatly appreciative of the opportunity to work with them to build a solid and workable website.
